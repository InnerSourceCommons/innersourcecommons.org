**🌐 This is a free, fully virtual event - join online from anywhere in the world.**
<br />
<br />

On Thursday, November 12th, InnerSource Summit 2026 will unite the global InnerSource community online for a 22-hour around-the-world event spanning every timezone!  We will be featuring the industry’s top speakers, inspiring stories, and cutting-edge ideas shaping the future of collaborative software development. 
<br />
<br />
Discover the Power of InnerSource!
<br />
<br />
InnerSource applies the proven principles of open source collaboration inside organizations — helping teams break down silos, accelerate innovation, improve developer experience, and build stronger engineering cultures.
<br />
<br />
Hosted by InnerSource Commons, the world’s leading community dedicated to advancing InnerSource practices, this year’s summit will feature top speakers and real-world stories from across the globe.
<br />
<br />
Whether you’re just beginning your InnerSource journey or scaling it across the enterprise, InnerSource Summit 2026 is the place to learn from the very best in the community.
<br />
<br />
Join participants worldwide to:
<br />
✨ Discover the latest trends shaping the future of InnerSource
<br />
✨ Hear inspiring stories from organizations transforming the way they collaborate
<br />
✨ Learn practical strategies, tools, and lessons from real-world implementations
<br />
✨ Connect with a global community passionate about open collaboration and innovation
<br />
✨ Participate in sessions happening around the clock across every region and timezone
<br />
✨ Build connections that will continue beyond the virtual summit through upcoming in-person InnerSource events in Spring 2027
<br />
<br />
From engineering leaders and open source experts to developers, program offices, and community builders, this summit is where the global InnerSource community comes together to share ideas, spark innovation, and shape the future of collaborative software development.
<br />
<br />
Be part of the movement.
<br />
Be part of the conversation.
<br />
Be part of InnerSource Summit 2026. 
<br />
<br />
Remember if you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together!
