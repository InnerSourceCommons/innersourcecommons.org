Over the past year, the InnerSource Commons community has continued to strengthen and mature InnerSource practices across organizations and industries. The State of InnerSource 2025 Report captures this progress, highlighting how InnerSource remains rooted in collaboration, openness, and transparency while becoming increasingly embedded within enterprise environments.
 
This year's findings outline why organizations invest in InnerSource, where measurable progress is being made, and the cultural and structural challenges that remain. The report also points to emerging areas of practice, including early signals at the intersection of InnerSource and generative AI. Explore the report to learn more about how InnerSource is evolving.
