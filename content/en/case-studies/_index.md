### [United Nations: Reboot UN-tapped](/case-studies/united-nations/)

The UN Secretariat ran an eight-week accelerator on InnerSource principles, drawing 100 staff from more than 40 entities to build solutions to real peacekeeping challenges. Two solutions are now being implemented in missions in Cyprus and South Sudan.

### [Microsoft: DevOps Dojo](/stories/microsoft/)

Microsoft's DevOps Dojo used InnerSource to create a single source of truth for customer-facing content, empowering a global community of practitioners across 36 countries to co-create and continuously improve it.
